Traditional Onam Games

Vadamvali (Tug of War)

The ultimate test of strength and teamwork! Teams pull with all their might in this thrilling contest.

Musical Chair

A classic game of speed and timing. Race for a seat when the music stops. Last one standing wins!

Sack Race

Hop your way to the finish line in this hilarious and challenging race. Balance is key!

Lemon & Spoon Race

A true test of balance and concentration. Can you make it to the end without dropping the lemon?

Pottu Thodal

A fun game of precision! While blindfolded, try to place the 'pottu' (bindi) in the right spot on a picture.

Saree Draping Contest

A race against time! Teams compete to see who can drape a saree perfectly in the shortest time.
